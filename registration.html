Registration
Domestic registration link: https://www.ais.cn/attendees/index/ZB3MZZ?invited=MIND
International registration link: https://paper-sub.com/register/ZB3MZZ
Registration fee
After September 15, 2025
• 2900 CNY abstract registration
• 2900 CNY regular attendee with no paper
• 2500 CNY student with no paper
Before September 15, 2025
• 3800 CNY regular registration with 1 paper (A)
• 3400 CNY student registration with 1 paper (B)
• 2500 CNY abstract registration
• 2500 CNY regular attendee with no paper
• 2100 CNY student with no paper
Extra paper fee: 1000 CNY for each more paper (A or B)
Exchange rate (USD): 7.2
(* Registration with accepted paper(s) should be completed by September 15, 2025)
The registration fee includes:
• Keynotes and Technical Sessions (November 1-2, 2025)
• Tutorials (Friday, October 31, 2025)
• Coffee Breaks (October 31-November 2, 2025)
• Lunch Buffets (November 1-2, 2025)
• Welcome Reception (Friday, October 31, 2025)
• Conference Banquet (Saturday, November 1, 2025)
• Paper Processing Fees (Applicable to A and B)
Registration policy
1.Author registration: At least one author of each accepted paper must register for the conference no later than September 15, 2025. The registration will benefit from early-bird rates and provide access to special hotel discounts with limited availability on a first-come, first-served basis. If, after having registered, an author or co-author of a paper is unexpectedly NOT available to present the paper at the conference due to, e.g., a visa issue, please contact zhanglvyin@ais.cn and we will try to find a solution on a case-by-case basis.
2.Cancellations and refund: Cancellation requests received on or before October 15, 2025, will be subject to a processing fee of 30% of the original registration fee. No refunds will be issued for cancellations made after October 15, 2025. All cancellation and refund requests must be submitted via email to zhanglvyin@ais.cn and received by the stated deadline.
3.Eating restrictions: During registration, please indicate your dietary restrictions. All food functions will have vegetarian items. We are striving to provide diverse culinary options; however, we may not be able to meet your dietary restrictions.
Please contact zhanglvyin@ais.cn if you have any other questions about registration. All requests for selection items change, after the completion of registrations, must be provided in writing by email to zhanglvyin@ais.cn.
