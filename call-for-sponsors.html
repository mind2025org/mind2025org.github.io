Call For Sponsors
Join MIND 2025 as a Sponsor to Co-create the Future of Intelligence
The 2025 International Conference on Machine Intelligence and Nature-Inspired Computing (MIND 2025) will be held from October 31 to November 2, 2025, in Xiamen, China. As a leading international forum in the field of machine intelligence and nature-inspired computation, MIND 2025 will bring together world-renowned scholars, technical experts, and industry leaders to explore cutting-edge trends and innovative applications in intelligent technologies.
We warmly invite companies from all sectors to join MIND 2025 as sponsors. Through this platform, your organization can increase brand visibility, showcase innovative solutions, expand collaboration networks, and connect with top research teams and emerging talent from around the world.
Sponsorship opportunities are available at three standard levels, i.e., Platinum, Gold, and Silver, with benefits such as brand exposure on the conference website and venue, inclusion in conference materials, complimentary registrations, exhibition space, and opportunities for technical interaction.
In addition to standard packages, MIND 2025 also welcomes customized sponsorship arrangements. For organizations with specific needs or partnership ideas, tailored sponsorship plans can be negotiated individually to maximize mutual value.
Please feel free to contact the organizing committee to explore sponsorship opportunities. We look forward to working with you to shape the future of intelligent technology!
